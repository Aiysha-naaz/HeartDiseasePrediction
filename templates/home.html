About Our System

Your heart beats tirelessly, keeping you alive every moment. But what if you could predict and prevent heart disease before it becomes a threat? That is exactly what our system helps you do.

Using machine learning and data analysis, our tool examines key health factors like age, blood pressure, and cholesterol to assess your risk of heart disease. It goes beyond just detection by providing insights into different types of heart diseases, their symptoms, causes, and essential health tips to help you stay informed and take action.
